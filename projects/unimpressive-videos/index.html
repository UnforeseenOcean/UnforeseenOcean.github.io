So what is Unimpressive Videos?

It is a channel dedicated to uploading videos that don't fit well in the world of perfectionism -- the uploader is trying his best, but his best simply isn't enough to satisfy the crowd.

It started one day in 2022, when I realized that I won't ever be good enough to reach the heights others are reaching. Rather than just giving up and dragging the recording to the recycling bin, I decided to make a new channel and upload some failures that most people won't see.

The channel was created with hopes of telling the beginners and others that everyone's imperfect and striving towards the ideal state simply leads to depression and agony.

It was also created to say that people like me exist and that everyone starts somewhere.
